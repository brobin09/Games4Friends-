MAKING THE VOICE SOUND HUMAN
============================

Three things changed, in order of how much they help.

1. RECORD YOUR OWN VOICE  (the real fix)
   Tap the 🎙️ button in the top bar. A grown-up gate appears (tap the number it
   asks for) so a toddler cannot wander in. Then for each line: tap Rec, say it,
   tap Stop. It plays back immediately. Recordings are saved on the iPad in
   IndexedDB and survive closing the app.

   If you only record a few, record ONE through FIVE. Those are said constantly,
   and they are what the countdown uses when the candles go out.

   The game plays your recording whenever one exists and falls back to the
   synthetic voice when it does not. You can mix and match freely.

2. DOWNLOAD A BETTER SYSTEM VOICE  (do this on the iPad, one time)
   Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices → English
   Download one of the ENHANCED or PREMIUM voices (Samantha (Enhanced) is the
   safe pick). The default "compact" voices are the robotic ones; the enhanced
   ones are several hundred megabytes of real recorded speech and sound
   dramatically better. The game automatically scores and picks the best voice
   installed, and de-prioritizes anything named Compact or Eloquence.

   You can also override the pick manually in the 🎙️ studio, with a Test button.

3. PROSODY  (already applied, no setup)
   - Long lines are split at sentence breaks and spoken as separate utterances
     with a pause between, instead of one flat run-on contour.
   - Counting now climbs in pitch and slows down, the way people actually count.
   - Exclamations lift the pitch; questions drop and slow.
   These are the only levers the Web Speech API gives; there is no SSML on iOS.

DEPLOY
   Cache version is now count-with-me-v2, so the iPad picks up the update.
